About Millie's

Nestled in the beautiful Houston neighborhood of Oak Forest, Millie's was created and designed to offer the best Coastal and Local flavors alike, along with a warm and charming environment that pays homage to the Coastal Charthouses that flourished in times past.

Our menu features fresh seafood, handcrafted cocktails, and locally-inspired dishes that celebrate the Gulf Coast's rich culinary heritage. Every dish is prepared with care using the finest ingredients available.
